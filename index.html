Privacy Policy — ResumeCraft

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

ResumeCraft is a resume builder application developed by Garvansh Gupta. This Privacy Policy explains how ResumeCraft handles user information when you use the app.

1. Information We Process

ResumeCraft allows you to create resumes, cover letters, and related career documents. The app may process information that you enter yourself, such as:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and address
  • Education details and work experience
  • Projects, skills, certifications, and languages
  • Awards and references
  • Profile photo, if added by you
  • Resume and cover letter content

This information is used only to help you create, preview, export, and manage your resumes inside the app.

2. Local Storage

ResumeCraft is designed as an offline-first app. Your resume data is stored locally on your device using local app storage.

We do not upload your resume data to our own servers.

3. Data Sharing

ResumeCraft does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data may leave the app only when you choose to perform an action such as:

  • Exporting a resume as PDF, PNG, or JSON
  • Sharing a resume using your device share menu
  • Saving files to your device storage
  • Importing or exporting backup files selected by you

In these cases, the data is handled according to the apps or services you choose to share it with.

4. Permissions

ResumeCraft may request device permissions only when needed for app functionality, such as:

  • Accessing files for importing resume JSON backups
  • Saving exported files to your device
  • Selecting or using a profile photo

These permissions are used only for the feature you choose to use.

5. Third-Party Services

ResumeCraft may use limited third-party services for app functionality and stability, such as:

  • Google Play services for app review prompts or platform features
  • Sentry for crash and error reporting, to help diagnose app crashes

If crash reporting is enabled, technical diagnostic information such as device model, app version, operating system version, and crash logs may be collected. ResumeCraft does not intentionally include resume content in crash reports.

6. Children's Privacy

ResumeCraft is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Since ResumeCraft stores resume information locally on your device, you are responsible for keeping your device secure.

Please avoid sharing exported resume files with unknown or untrusted apps or people.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete your resume data from within the app by removing resumes or clearing app data. You can also remove exported files manually from your device storage.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored app data may be deleted according to your device's operating system behavior.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
